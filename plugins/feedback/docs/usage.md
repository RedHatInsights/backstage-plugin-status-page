# Usage

## Global Feedbacks

These are the feedbacks collected for complete platform. The collected feedback gets stored in database and creates a ticket on issue tracker service
which can be configured by app owner.

To submit feedback for application

1. Click on the **Send Feedback** button bubble at bottom right corner of backstage.
2. Click on **Bug** or **Share Feedback** button.
3. Select the **Tag**. Fill in **Summary** and click on **Submit** button.

To view your submitted feedback, Goto the **Feedback page** from the sidebar menu.

## Entity Feedbacks
These are the feedbacks for individual catalog entity, 

To submit feedback for a component

1. Click on any component from Backstage Catalog.
2. Goto **Feedback** tab.
3. Click on **Create** button.
4. Select your feedback type between **Issue/Feedback**.
5. Select the tag.
6. Fill in **Summary** and **Description** fields.
7. Click on **Submit** button

Global feedbacks are also a part of catalog entity and assigned to a component configurable.
